About Us

We are a five-member team of first-year DAW (Web Application Development) students at the San Valero Center in Zaragoza, Spain. We developed this website as a project for the NASA Space App Challenge, choosing the Stellar Stories: Space Weather Through the Eyes of Earthlings challenge. We chose this challenge because it was a good fit for what we could develop with our web development skills. We used HTML, CSS, and JS, as these are the technologies we were familiar with.
